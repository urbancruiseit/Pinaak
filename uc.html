FAQ’s Frequently Asked Questions

What types of vehicles are available for rental in Delhi?

Urban Cruise offers comprehensive vehicle rental options, including economy sedans, luxury cars, tempo travellers, Maharaja tempo travellers, Force Urbania vans, mini buses, luxury coaches, sleeper buses, and Volvo buses. Seating capacities range from 4-seater cars to 56-seater buses. Every vehicle category features multiple variants with different amenity levels suited for budget-conscious travellers and luxury seekers.

How can I book a vehicle rental in Delhi?

Booking vehicles remains simple through our website or customer service. Visit urbancruise.in, select your preferred vehicle on rent, and enter travel details including pickup location, dates, and destination. Review transparent pricing, compare vehicle features, and confirm booking with secure payment. Instant confirmation includes driver contact details and vehicle information. Alternatively, call & speak with travel advisors who assist with personalized vehicle rental service recommendations.

What are the rental rates for vehicles in Delhi?

Vehicle rental rates vary based on type, seating capacity, and amenities. Economy cars start from ₹12/km while luxury car rentals start from ₹50/km. Tempo traveller on rent starts from ₹18/km depending on seating. Maharaja tempo traveller per km rate begins at ₹23/km while Force Urbania rent per km starts ₹28/km. luxury Bus rental per km starts at ₹40/km based on size and luxury level. Rates exclude tolls, parking, state permits, and driver allowances. Monthly packages and corporate contracts receive special discounted pricing.

Can I rent vehicles for outstation trips from Delhi?

Absolutely! Urban Cruise specializes in outstation car rental to popular destinations including Agra, Jaipur, Shimla, Manali, Rishikesh, Haridwar, Jim Corbett, and other North Indian locations. Vehicles undergo thorough pre-trip inspections while drivers receive route briefings. Multi-day packages accommodate extended vacations with flexible itineraries.

What is the difference between regular and Maharaja tempo travellers?

Regular tempo traveller on rent feature 2×2 seating arrangement maximizing passenger capacity with basic amenities. Maharaja tempo traveller on rent offers exclusive 1×1 extra-wide Maharaja seating providing individual space, premium reclining seats with adjustable headrests, armrests, and legrests. Maharaja tempo traveller on rent includes superior entertainment systems, ambient lighting, climate control, and luxury upholstery. While regular tempo travellers suit budget-conscious groups, the luxury maharaja tempo traveller delivers first-class comfort ideal for VIP travel.

Are vehicle rentals available for corporate events?

Yes, we provide comprehensive commercial vehicle rental and corporate transportation solutions including employee daily commutes, client meetings, conference transportation, team outings, and executive travel. Fleet options span economy cars for individual executives to luxury buses for large team events. Professional drivers trained in corporate protocols maintain punctuality and confidentiality.

What is the difference between luxury cars and luxury vans?

Luxury car rental features Luxury Car on rent and SUVs on rent with 4-7 passenger capacity ideal for executive travel, business meetings, and VIP transportation. Luxury van rental includes vehicles like Mercedes V-Class and Toyota Vellfire with 6-9 passengers offering captain seats, spacious interiors, and premium amenities. Luxury vans suit small group luxury travel while maintaining an intimate atmosphere. Both categories represent premium transportation but differ in passenger capacity and interior configurations.
